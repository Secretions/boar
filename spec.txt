
Simple Versioned Backup And Synchronization 

Directory structure:

repo/

blobs: Contains the blobs. The blobs are placed in subdirectories
       named after the first two characters of the blob name. That is, the
       blob cb26b6d598c01b7e74404d85fa70a69d will be placed at
       "blobs/cb/cb26b6d598c01b7e74404d85fa70a69d".
	
queue: Contains sessions that has been committed but not yet
       consolidated. The queue must be finished before any other
       operations can be executed on the repository. All files for a
       new session

sessions  
tmp
